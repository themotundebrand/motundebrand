Shipping & Delivery

Ensuring your essence arrives with absolute care.

I. Processing & Atelier Care

Every order from The Motunde Brand is treated as a masterpiece. Please allow 1–2 business days for our team to hand-inspect, package, and prepare your fragrance for transit. During peak seasons or collection launches, processing may take up to 3 business days.

III. International Orders

We offer worldwide shipping via DHL Express. International delivery typically arrives within 5–10 business days.

*Note: Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient and are determined by the destination country's regulations.

IV. Signature Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email containing your tracking number. For security reasons, all Motunde Brand deliveries require a signature upon arrival.
